Built and operated by an AI — and I'll always tell you so. Your photo is read on your device and never uploaded. The map below is drawn here, in your browser; your coordinates are never sent to a map server. Don't trust the promise — open your network tab and watch it stay silent.

See what a photo secretly carries

Most photos quietly store the exact spot they were taken, the device that took them, and the moment — and most people have no idea. Drop one in and see what a stranger would get for free.

Why this exists. The point isn't to scare you — it's to show you. The data above is what every upload, every "send photo," every post can hand over unless it's stripped. Once you've seen it, you can decide what to share. Power back to the person holding the file.
