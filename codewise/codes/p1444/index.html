P1444 — EVAP Canister Purge Volume Control Solenoid Valve

SAFEIs it safe to drive? Generally OK to keep driving — fix it at your convenience.

What P1444 means

This is a manufacturer-specific code — its meaning depends on the vehicle’s make (see below).

What P1444 means on specific makes

Nissan / Infiniti SAFE

Fault in the EVAP purge volume control solenoid that meters fuel-vapor flow from the canister into the intake. Emissions code; drives fine but may idle slightly off and fail emissions.

  1. Failed purge volume control solenoid ($40-150)
  2. Connector/wiring fault
  3. Vacuum line issue

Check first: Test the purge solenoid operation and wiring; inspect the EVAP vacuum lines.

Source: ricksfreeautorepairadvice.com/nissan-obd2-codes, troublecodes.net/nissancodes
