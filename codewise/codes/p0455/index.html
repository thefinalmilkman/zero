P0455 — EVAP Large Leak Detected

SAFEIs it safe to drive? A big fuel-vapor leak. Not a drivability hazard; will fail emissions and you may smell fuel.

What P0455 means

A large EVAP leak — most often a gas cap that's loose, missing, or not sealing.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Gas cap off / loose / failed seal
  2. Disconnected or split EVAP hose
  3. Stuck-open purge or vent valve
  4. Damaged filler neck

Symptoms you might notice

  • Check-engine light
  • Fuel smell, especially after fill-ups

What to check first

Confirm the cap seals; inspect EVAP lines for a disconnect or split. Smoke-test to pin a large leak quickly.
