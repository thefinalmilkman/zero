P0743 — Torque Converter Clutch Solenoid Circuit Electrical

CAUTIONIs it safe to drive? The TCC solenoid has an electrical fault (open or short). Lockup won't function correctly — economy suffers and the transmission works harder at cruise.

What P0743 means

Unlike P0741 (performance) or P0742 (stuck on), P0743 is a confirmed electrical circuit fault in the TCC solenoid wiring or the solenoid itself. Usually a simpler fix than internal mechanical issues.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Failed TCC solenoid (open or shorted coil)
  2. Wiring fault — open circuit or short in the solenoid wiring
  3. Corroded connector at the solenoid or TCM
  4. Low or contaminated fluid fouling the solenoid

Symptoms you might notice

  • No TCC lockup at cruise
  • High RPM at highway speed
  • Poor fuel economy

What to check first

Measure the TCC solenoid resistance at the trans connector (typically 10-20 ohms — check your make's spec). Open (OL) or near-zero = solenoid. Correct resistance = wiring or TCM.
