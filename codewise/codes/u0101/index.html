U0101 — Lost Communication With TCM

STOPIs it safe to drive? The engine computer lost contact with the transmission control module. The transmission will likely go into limp mode — don't drive this until the cause is found.

What U0101 means

This is a network code, not a transmission fault: the TCM stopped responding on the CAN bus. Before assuming the TCM is bad, check its fuses, power supply, and grounds — those fail far more often than the module itself. A CAN bus fault also triggers this.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Blown TCM fuse or lost power/ground to the TCM
  2. CAN bus wiring fault between the TCM and gateway
  3. Corroded or damaged TCM connector
  4. Failed TCM (less common than power/ground issues)

Symptoms you might notice

  • Transmission stuck in limp mode
  • No communication with TCM on scan tool
  • Multiple trans codes set

What to check first

Pull the TCM fuses and check them. Verify power and ground at the TCM connector with the key on. If power/ground are good and you can't communicate with the TCM, check CAN-H/CAN-L continuity to the gateway before replacing the module.
