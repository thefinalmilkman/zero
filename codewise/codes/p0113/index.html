P0113 — Intake Air Temp (IAT) Sensor High Input

SAFEIs it safe to drive? A temperature-sensor circuit fault. Minor drivability effect; fix at convenience.

What P0113 means

The intake-air-temperature signal reads as very cold (high resistance / open circuit). The computer uses IAT for fine fuel/timing trim.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. IAT sensor connector / wiring (open circuit)
  2. Failed IAT sensor
  3. Often built into the MAF — bad MAF

Symptoms you might notice

  • Check-engine light
  • Slightly off cold-start behavior

What to check first

Check the IAT reading against actual ambient temp; inspect the connector. If integrated into the MAF, the MAF is the part.
