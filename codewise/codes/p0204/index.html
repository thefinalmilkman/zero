P0204 — Injector Circuit Malfunction — Cylinder 4

STOPIs it safe to drive? Do not continue driving — a dead injector on any cylinder causes misfire damage to the catalytic converter rapidly; get it diagnosed promptly.

What P0204 means

Electrical fault in the cylinder 4 fuel injector circuit. May be an open, short, or resistance out of range. On 4-cylinder engines this is particularly impactful as it represents 25% of the power output.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Failed fuel injector (open or shorted)
  2. Loose or corroded injector connector — especially common on high-mileage 4-cylinder engines where the harness is well-worn
  3. Open in wiring harness to cylinder 4
  4. Heat damage to wiring near the exhaust manifold (cylinder 4 is often closest to the downpipe)
  5. PCM injector driver failure

Symptoms you might notice

  • Misfire on cylinder 4 (P0304 companion code common)
  • Strong vibration at idle on 4-cylinder engines
  • Significant power loss
  • Possible popping or backfire
  • Fuel or rich smell from exhaust if injector stuck open

What to check first

Perform the swap test: swap the cylinder 4 injector connector with cylinder 3. If the code follows to P0203, the harness wiring is the fault. If it stays P0204, the injector is faulty. On 4-cylinder engines, also check for heat damage on the wiring near the number 4 exhaust port since that corner of the engine runs hottest.
