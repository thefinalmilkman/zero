P1505 — Idle Air Control (IAC) System at Adaptive Clip

CAUTIONIs it safe to drive? Drive gently and get it looked at soon — it can worsen or fail inspection.

What P1505 means

This is a manufacturer-specific code — its meaning depends on the vehicle’s make (see below).

What P1505 means on specific makes

Ford / Lincoln CAUTION

The PCM has run the idle-air-control adaptation to its limit ('clip') trying to keep idle correct — it's out of adjustment range. Typically a dirty/sticking IAC valve or an unmetered air (vacuum) leak. Common on older Fords with a separate IAC.

  1. Dirty/sticking IAC valve ($30-120)
  2. Vacuum leak ($0-200)
  3. Dirty throttle body ($0-30 to clean)
  4. PCV system fault ($20-80)

Check first: Clean the IAC valve and throttle body, then smoke-test for vacuum leaks.

Source: TroubleCodes.net Ford list
