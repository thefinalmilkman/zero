P0606 — PCM / ECM Processor Fault

CAUTIONIs it safe to drive? The engine computer flagged an internal fault. Can cause erratic running; have it checked — but verify power/grounds before condemning a computer.

What P0606 means

The powertrain control module reported an internal processor or self-test failure. Real ECM failures happen, but bad grounds and low voltage cause this far more often.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Poor ECM ground or power supply (check FIRST)
  2. Corroded ECM connector
  3. Water intrusion
  4. Actual ECM internal failure (rare)

Symptoms you might notice

  • Erratic running / stalling
  • Multiple unrelated codes
  • No-start

What to check first

Before buying a computer: clean and verify all ECM grounds and power feeds, inspect the connectors for corrosion/water. Most 'bad ECM' codes are really power/ground problems.
