C0050 — Right Rear Wheel Speed Sensor Circuit

CAUTIONIs it safe to drive? ABS, traction, and stability are off — regular brakes work normally. Drive with extra following distance.

What C0050 means

Same principle as C0045 on the right rear. On rear-wheel-drive vehicles the sensor is often near the differential; on front-wheel-drive it's at the trailing arm. Rust and road debris damage are the leading causes on older vehicles.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Corroded or cracked harness at the right rear
  2. Failed wheel speed sensor
  3. Damaged tone ring or bearing
  4. Harness abrasion on suspension or brake components

Symptoms you might notice

  • ABS light on
  • Traction and stability off

What to check first

Get the car on a lift and trace the entire right-rear harness from the sensor to the body. Look for cracked insulation, missing harness clips, and green corrosion in the connector.
