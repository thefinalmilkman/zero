P0303 — Cylinder 3 Misfire Detected

STOPIs it safe to drive? Cylinder 3 is misfiring. Don't drive hard on a flashing light — the cat pays for it.

What P0303 means

Cylinder 3 failing to fire cleanly; isolated, so it's that cylinder's ignition, fuel, or mechanical health.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Plug or coil on cylinder 3
  2. Injector for cylinder 3
  3. Low compression on cylinder 3
  4. Local vacuum leak

Symptoms you might notice

  • Rough idle
  • Hesitation
  • Fuel smell

What to check first

Swap parts with an adjacent cylinder to isolate; compression-test if spark+fuel check out.
