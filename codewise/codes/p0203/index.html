P0203 — Injector Circuit Malfunction — Cylinder 3

STOPIs it safe to drive? A dead cylinder on cylinder 3 will cause rough running and rapid catalytic converter damage — diagnose before driving more than necessary.

What P0203 means

Electrical fault detected in the cylinder 3 injector circuit. The injector may be getting no signal, no power, or may be shorted and drawing too much current.

Most likely causes (in order)

  1. Failed fuel injector for cylinder 3
  2. Open circuit in wiring or a corroded connector at cylinder 3
  3. Short to ground on the injector signal wire
  4. Wiring harness damage (heat or abrasion near exhaust or valve cover)
  5. PCM driver failure (uncommon)

Symptoms you might notice

  • Misfire on cylinder 3 (P0303 typically also set)
  • Engine vibration at idle
  • Loss of power
  • Possible raw fuel smell if injector is stuck open

What to check first

Use a noid light — a small test light that plugs into the injector harness connector while the engine cranks. If the noid light pulses, the ECU is commanding the injector; resistance-test the injector itself to confirm it's bad. If the noid light doesn't pulse, the fault is upstream — wiring, fuse, or PCM driver.
