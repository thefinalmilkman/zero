Butylene Glycol

Broadly well-tolerated

Function: Humectant / solvent

Is Butylene Glycol safe in skincare?
Broadly well-tolerated. Considered a low-concern ingredient for most people at the levels used in cosmetics.

What it does

A humectant and solvent that also helps other ingredients absorb.

The honest read

Well-tolerated by most.

Who should pay attention

If you have sensitive or reactive skin:Occasionally irritating to very sensitive skin.
Sources: CIR · A code or a name is hazard, not risk — what matters is the dose actually used.
