Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane (Avobenzone)

Can irritate reactive skin

Function: UV filter

Is Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane safe in skincare?
Generally safe, but it can sting or irritate reactive skin, especially at higher levels.

What it does

A chemical sunscreen filter that absorbs UVA rays.

The honest read

Effective; needs stabilizing partners and can occasionally irritate.

Who should pay attention

If you have sensitive or reactive skin:An occasional sensitizer, even though it is fragrance-free.
Sources: FDA; EU CosIng · A code or a name is hazard, not risk — what matters is the dose actually used.
