Benzyl Alcohol

Use-restricted — safe within limits

Function: Preservative / solvent

Is Benzyl Alcohol safe in skincare?
Use-restricted, not unsafe. Regulators allow it within set concentration limits — the dose is the point, and products stay inside those limits.

What it does

A mild preservative and solvent (also one of the EU-labelled fragrance allergens).

The honest read

Usually well-tolerated; flagged only because it doubles as a fragrance allergen.

Who should pay attention

If you're sensitive to fragrance:Counts among the labelled fragrance allergens at higher levels.
If you have sensitive or reactive skin:Rarely irritating.
Sources: EU Annex III · A code or a name is hazard, not risk — what matters is the dose actually used.
