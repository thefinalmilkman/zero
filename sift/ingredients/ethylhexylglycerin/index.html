Ethylhexylglycerin

Broadly well-tolerated

Function: Preservative booster

Is Ethylhexylglycerin safe in skincare?
Broadly well-tolerated. Considered a low-concern ingredient for most people at the levels used in cosmetics.

What it does

Helps preservatives work and softens skin; usually paired with phenoxyethanol.

The honest read

Generally well-tolerated.

Who should pay attention

If you have sensitive or reactive skin:An occasional irritant in very reactive skin.
Sources: CIR · A code or a name is hazard, not risk — what matters is the dose actually used.
