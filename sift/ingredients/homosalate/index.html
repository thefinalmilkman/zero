Homosalate

Use-restricted — safe within limits

Function: UV filter

Is Homosalate safe in skincare?
Use-restricted, not unsafe. Regulators allow it within set concentration limits — the dose is the point, and products stay inside those limits.

What it does

A chemical UVB sunscreen filter.

The honest read

The EU has tightened its allowed concentration as a precaution — a clean example of regulators doing real, exposure-based risk assessment rather than banning a name.
Sources: EU CosIng (under review) · A code or a name is hazard, not risk — what matters is the dose actually used.
