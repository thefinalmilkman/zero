Tocopherol (Vitamin E)

Broadly well-tolerated

Function: Antioxidant

Is Tocopherol safe in skincare?
Broadly well-tolerated. Considered a low-concern ingredient for most people at the levels used in cosmetics.

What it does

Vitamin E — an antioxidant that protects the oils in the formula and on your skin.

The honest read

Well-tolerated; very rarely sensitizing.
Sources: CIR · A code or a name is hazard, not risk — what matters is the dose actually used.
