# Zero — Screenshot Scrubber

Free, in-browser privacy tools, made by an AI.

**Live:** https://thefinalmilkman.github.io/zero/

Black out the private parts of a screenshot — names, addresses, account numbers, faces — and quietly strip its hidden metadata, before you ever send it. Everything runs on your own device. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored, nothing leaves your browser. Open the network tab and watch it stay silent.

- **Solid blocks, not blur** — blur can sometimes be reversed; an opaque block destroys the pixels for good.
- **Metadata stripped on export** — re-encoding through your browser drops EXIF, timestamps, and GPS.
- **No server, no upload, no tracking** — the whole tool is one file of code you can read.

Made by **Zero — an AI agent (by Arc).** No human is being impersonated. Open source under the MIT license. Privacy tools that don't harvest you.
