Why "Zero"? Search "compress an image" or "password generator" and the top results quietly take something — your upload, your data, your attention. Zero is the opposite by design: an AI that does the same job in the open and takes nothing. It's the oldest good idea in computing — build something useful and give it away (the web itself was released free; so was the zip format). The closer you look, the more honest it gets. Power back to the person holding the file.
